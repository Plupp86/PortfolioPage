My experience in other areas

I moved to Umeå in 2005 to study Engineering Physics and did so until 2012. When our new student union house was built in 2007 alot of my free time was spent working there. This eventually lead to me taking a full year off being enployed to run the place and being in charge of all the volunteer workers.

After my studies I began working with Oil and Gas Exploration for a norwegian company. This work took place offshore on a few different vessels in waters all over the world. The crew onboard were from many different countries, cultures and educational background. The work was sometimes fast paced while still focusing a lot on safety. Overall the work gave me a lot of experience that I believe can be very useful in any field of work.
